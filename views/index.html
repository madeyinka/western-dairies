About Us

At Western Dairies, we organize small holding cattle and goat dairy farmers into cooperatives and aggregate their milks for production of fresh milk, cheese and butter.

Our Research, Development and Extension Hub manages several dairy science projects which are integrated with dairy extension and communication.

We invest in unique locally-based research activities including pasture seed performance, smarter irrigation and dairy effluent management, help increase farmer knowledge and improve business productivity and profitability.

Western Dairy values relationships with small holding dairy farmers as an enterprise-driven approach to livelihood enhancement as well as an instrument of rural poverty reduction. We believe that smallholder dairying can bring significant social as well as environmental benefits for the people.
